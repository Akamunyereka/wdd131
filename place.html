Welcome to Japan

Japan is an island nation in East Asia, known for its unique blend of ancient traditions and cutting-edge technology. From serene temples to bustling cities, Japan offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

General Information

  • Capital: Tokyo
  • Population: 125.7 million
  • Language: Japanese
  • Currency: Japanese Yen (¥)
  • Area: 377,975 km²

Top Attractions

  • Mount Fuji - Japan's iconic sacred mountain
  • Fushimi Inari Shrine - Famous for thousands of vermillion torii gates
  • Tokyo Tower - Symbol of Japan's modern era
  • Hiroshima Peace Memorial - Historic WWII monument
  • Kyoto's Golden Pavilion - Stunning Zen Buddhist temple

Culture & Traditions

Japanese culture is renowned worldwide for its rich traditions including:

  • Tea Ceremony (Chanoyu)
  • Cherry Blossom Viewing (Hanami)
  • Traditional Arts (Ikebana, Calligraphy)
  • Martial Arts (Judo, Kendo, Aikido)
  • Traditional Theatre (Kabuki, Noh)
