Campus Fitness Hub – Website Planning Document

Site Name

Campus Fitness Hub

The name represents a central online resource for students seeking fitness programs, workout plans, and wellness tips on campus. It communicates energy, health, and community.

Optional domain availability: campusfitnesshub.com

Site Purpose

The Campus Fitness Hub website will provide students with workout routines, nutrition tips, gym schedules, and a contact form for fitness coaching inquiries. The site will serve as a central hub for promoting health and wellness within the campus community.

Scenarios

  • What workout plan should I follow as a beginner?
  • Where can I find the campus gym schedule?
  • How can I contact a fitness trainer for personalized advice?

Color Schema

  • Primary Color: #1B4332 (Dark Green) – Used for headings, navigation, and footer background.
  • Accent Color: #2D6A4F (Medium Green) – Used for buttons, highlights, and section borders.
  • Background Color: #F8F9FA (Light Gray) – Used for page background.

Typography

  • Montserrat – Used for headings (h1, h2).
  • Open Sans – Used for body text and paragraphs.
